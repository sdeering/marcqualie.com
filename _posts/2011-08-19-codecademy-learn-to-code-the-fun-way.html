--- layout: post title: "Codecademy: Learn to Code the fun way" date: 2011-08-19 09:26:00 tags: --- This morning, I just stumbled across a cool site which teaches people who to code, without it being an entirely tedious task. The site tries to engage people by actually letting them code in a console window while they are learning, getting feedback as they progress through the stages. The site is http://www.codecademy.com/ and I advise you try it out if you're wanting to get into basic coding, or you just want to play around with an interactive console window for a while.
