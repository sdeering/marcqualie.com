--- layout: post title: "Class3 Xbox Live Exclusive" date: 2011-02-03 23:03:00 tags: --- Undead labs have just released information regarding their upcoming title “Class3″. They have entered a publishing deal with Microsoft Game Studios to develop this new online, ever changing zombie survival horror game. Undead Labs will use Class3 as a platform to develop its other unannounced project, Class4, a persistent online game, (not an MMO) and will released at a later date.

This game excites me personally due to my love of zombie survival games, such as Left 4 Dead. I love the idea of having a persistent world that changes as more players join and work together. You can read the full article at the source below.
